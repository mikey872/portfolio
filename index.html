Frequently Asked Questions

Most asked questions about me and my services.

1. 1. What's the difference between On-Page and Off-Page SEO?

On-Page SEO focuses on optimizing elements within your website—like meta tags, content, and structure—to improve search rankings. Off-Page SEO, on the other hand, involves activities outside your website, such as link-building, guest posting, and social media signals, to boost your site's authority.

2. How does Technical SEO impact my website's performance?

Technical SEO ensures that your website is fast, mobile-friendly, and easily crawlable by search engines. These improvements help search engines index your pages more efficiently, which can lead to better rankings and a smoother user experience.

3. Why is Local SEO important for my business?

Local SEO makes it easier for clients in your area to locate you by promoting your company in local search results and maps. For physical businesses looking to boost foot traffic and local awareness, it's quite beneficial.

4. How often should I conduct an SEO audit?

A thorough SEO audit should be conducted at least every three to six months. This aids in tracking performance, identifying and resolving technical or content-related problems, and modifying tactics to stay abreast of changes to search engine algorithms.

5. What does Content Optimization include?

Making your website's content more interesting, keyword-rich, and audience-relevant is known as content optimization. In addition to increasing user retention and conversion rates, this guarantees improved search engine presence.
